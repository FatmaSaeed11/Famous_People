His football career

Manchester United

Ronaldo became the first Portuguese player to sign Manchester United with a contract worth 15 million euros , Manchester United's Cristiano Ronaldo scored the 1000 goal in the club's Premier League history on November 29 .

Real Madrid

On 26 June 2009, Real Madrid confirmed that Cristiano Ronaldo will join the club on 1 July 2009 from Manchester United for 80 million pounds , Ronaldo began preparing for the first leg of the 2017 Spanish Super Cup against arch-rivals Barcelona in the Camp Nou .

Juventus

On July 10, 2018, the Italian club Juventus announced the inclusion of Portuguese striker Cristiano Ronaldo from Real Madrid for 105 million euros for a period of 4 years until 30 June 2022, with a salary of 30 million euros .
